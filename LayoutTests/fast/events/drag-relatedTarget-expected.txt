Test that dragenter and dragleave events carry the correct relatedTarget. Per the DOM Level 2 Events spec, dragenter.relatedTarget is the element just exited and dragleave.relatedTarget is the element being entered.

On success, you will see a series of "PASS" messages, followed by "TEST COMPLETE".
